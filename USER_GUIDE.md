# 🌟 Welcome to GyaanSeetu (ज्ञानसीतु)
### *Bridging Ancient Wisdom with Future Tech*

---

GyaanSeetu is a state-of-the-art, AI-powered learning ecosystem designed specifically for CBSE/NCERT Class 6–10 students. By harmonizing classical educational philosophy with futuristic artificial intelligence, GyaanSeetu transforms passive school studying into an interactive, career-aligned, and deeply enriching journey of self-growth.

---

## 🚀 What GyaanSeetu Provides

### 1. 🧠 Personalized AI Guru Mentorship
Every student has direct access to **Guru AI**, powered by Google Gemini. 
* **Custom Guidance:** Receives personalized encouragement and study insights based on their learning progress and career targets.
* **Soft Skills Dojo:** Interactive, real-time roleplay scenarios (Conflict Resolution, Public Speaking, Negotiation) that prepare students for real-world life challenges.
* **Daily Growth Dojo:** A daily curriculum of a "Word of the Day" with phonetic pronunciation, and a "Critical Thinking Essay" graded dynamically with constructive feedback.

### 2. 📚 Complete Interactive Curriculum
A modern, rich CBSE Class 6–10 syllabus covering **Mathematics, Science, English, and Social Science**.
* **Three-Level Video Streams:** Learn a single concept through **Concept-focused**, **Animated-visual**, or **Real-world application** lectures.
* **AI-Generated Chapter Quizzes:** Test your knowledge with dynamically generated, syllabus-specific multiple-choice tests created on the fly by Gemini.
* **Structured Revision Notes:** Clean, high-yield markdown-rendered reading material for every chapter.

### 3. 🧪 Virtual Simulation Labs
Science and mathematics come to life inside our interactive laboratory. Drag, drop, adjust, and simulate key physical concepts (like velocity, gravity, or geometry) in real-time, closing the gap between raw equations and physical reality.

### 4. 🗺️ Career Path Alignment
GyaanSeetu answers the age-old question: *"Why am I studying this?"*
* Select from 5 career tracks: **Software Engineer, Medical Professional, Research Scientist, Civil Services,** or **Digital Artist**.
* Our intelligent keyword system dynamically highlights chapters in the syllabus most relevant to that job and charts a personalized, step-by-step roadmap to your dream career.

### 5. 🤝 Moderated Peer-to-Peer Community
A safe, localized forum where students ask questions, upvote/downvote queries, share helpful tips, and view peer profiles. Teachers can verify correct replies with a distinct badge, keeping the community productive and secure.
